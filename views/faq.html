FAQs


What is HappenStance?

HappenStance is a conversation starter with everyone based on what you feel at the moment, you can start a conversation by sharing a message/picture or a video with people around or with a closed group at a location.
People would recieve your message/picture or video and contribute to the conversation when they are at the same location or are in the vacinity of that location.

Can I connect happenStance to Facebook? Can I delete friends?

Not yet, but we are definitely working on this functionality right now. Friend deletion will be available in happenStance 1.1.

HappenStance prerequisies?

It works on android devices running 2.2 or above and requires an Internet Connection.

How do start using HappenStance?

You can register with HappenStance to create your account and start using the service.

Does HappenStance run in the background, use GPS all the time?

HappenStance does not run in the background all the time.
