Privacy Policy

We store the geographical locations of happenStance (latitude/longitude). happenStance checks with our servers periodically to see if your current location puts you in range of a happenStance, but your location as you move about the Earth is not stored in any permanent way. we have a database of all of your conversations and their location, but we don't have a database containing where you've been.

We also do not share your information with anyone for any reason. We hate spammers and the like, and we protect your information as if it were our very own.
